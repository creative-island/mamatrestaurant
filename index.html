Our Story

E xperience the vibrant flavors of Nigeria at Mama T's, a proudly woman-owned restaurant in Newport, UK. We invite you to savor authentic Nigerian dishes, expertly crafted with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Whether you dine-in and soak in the warm ambiance or enjoy the convenience of delivery, we bring the heart of Nigerian cuisine to your table. Beyond delicious food, Mama T's specializes in creating unforgettable experiences. Our dedicated team excels in event planning and exquisite decor, transforming any occasion into a truly special celebration. At Mama T's, we believe that great food should be accompanied by exceptional service. Come discover why 'great food and unforgettable experiences' are always on the menu!"
